Hi, I am Indira!

MODEL | CONTENT CREATOR | POLE DANCER | MORE

London, UK

I am a model based in London who specializes in editorial and beauty shots and has a strong interest in creativity & high fashion.

I celebrate and love my identity as a model, pole dancer, and software developer who indulges in all types of creative hobbies and movement expressions.

I grew up in Romania as a very active kid with many passions:fashion, beauty, dancing, singing, and being in front of the camera. I come from a poor family, and since creative jobs used to pay next to nothing, I have always been force-taught that these must always remain hobbies. This is why, when I had the opportunity to try modeling as a teenager in Bucharest and was offered a 5-year contract, I had to refuse it.


I re-purposed my time and energy towards leaving the country for a better future. After completing a Computer Science Degree in Manchester, I landed a Software Developer job at one of the investment banks in London. Itried to fitin by inhibiting my passions. This reached a tipping point during the pandemic when all these soul shortcomings have grown into depression.

Everything was amplified when I developed a rare health condition, Sphincter of Oddi Dysfunction, which causes me episodes of excruciating pain. With this push from my body, I resumed nurturing my hobbies. Now, I want to take this further into a fully-fledged modeling career.


My goal is to show myself and the world that with determination, you can become a successful model at any age. Furthermore, I would like to draw from my experience and promote healthy standards in a historically flawed industry. I want the young girls looking at me to have an example of a sustainable and balanced lifestyle. I want to be part of a shift towards achieving our ideal body by finding the best type of movement and diet choices that fuel our souls and don't feel like a punishment.
